Services for Students

AMG Overseas Services has no standardized approach but rather works through a personalized approach to ensure the best possible service to suit every student's individual needs.

Student Counselling
AMG provides expert student counseling services to help you navigate the complex process of studying abroad. Our team of experts will work with you every step of the way, from academic assessments to finding the right university, scholarship opportunities, and financial planning.

We are committed to helping you achieve your academic and career goals, so don't hesitate to reach out to AMG for personalized, professional student counseling.
What We Discuss
Academic Assessment
Our team of experts works closely with students to match their academic backgrounds, interests, and career aspirations to the right university programs.
Chose a Suitable University
At AMG, we understand the importance of finding the right university, and we work closely with students to help them find the right fit.
Scholarship Opportunities
We help students find and apply for scholarships that are best suited to their needs and goals.
Financial Planning
We provide students with comprehensive financial planning services, including budgeting and cost estimation, funding options and loan advice, and more. Our team can help you plan for the costs associated with studying abroad and make the most of your investment in your future.

University Admission
Once you've selected your university, AMG Education Consultancy will work with you to guide you through the university admission process. Our team of experts will help you understand the requirements, deadlines, and procedures for admission, and will provide support and guidance throughout the entire process.

With AMG's assistance, you can be confident that your admission to your chosen university will be smooth and successful.
Our Process
• Bring the required documents.

• Chose a suitable university.

• Our counselor will process everything

• Accept the offer letter and
pay your tuition fee.

• Get your enrolment confirmation.
Visa Consultancy
After successful university admission, when you get an offer letter AMG will provide visa consultancy services to ensure that you have all the information and support you need to obtain your student visa. Our team of experts will guide you through the visa application process, providing information on requirements, documentation, and procedures.

With AMG's assistance, you can be confident that your visa application will be completed accurately and efficiently, allowing you to focus on your upcoming studies abroad.

Accommodation
Finding suitable accommodation can be a challenge when studying abroad. At AMG, we understand this and provide students with comprehensive accommodation advice. Our team of experts will help you find the right housing option for your needs and budget, whether you're interested in on-campus housing, off-campus apartments, or homestays.

With AMG's assistance, you can feel confident that you will find comfortable, convenient, and affordable accommodation for your study abroad experience.
What We Discuss
• Suitable Place to live.

• Living cost.

• Budgeting advice .
Pre-Departure Briefing
AMG understands that the transition to studying abroad can be overwhelming. That's why we provide comprehensive pre-departure briefings to help students prepare for their study abroad experience. Our team of experts will provide information on cultural differences, language and currency, transportation, and other important details to help ensure a smooth and successful transition.

With AMG's assistance, you can feel confident and well-prepared for your study abroad experience.
What We discuss
• Campus rules and regulations.

• International student visa regulations.

• International Travel Rules.

• Student-living essentials.

• Travel Plan.

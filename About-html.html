About AMG
AMG - Your Partner in Studying Abroad since 2003

AMG Overseas Services is an education consultancy that provides personalized support to students who aspire to study abroad. As the official representative of well-reputed universities in Australia, Canada, UK, USA, Germany, Switzerland, Singapore, China in Bangladesh, AMG is dedicated to helping students simplify and plan their study abroad aspirations.

At AMG, we believe that an informed decision is a good decision. That's why we provide students with accurate facts and guidelines to help them making informed decisions about their study abroad plans. Our personalized consultations are designed to cater to the specific needs of each student, ensuring that they receive the support they need to achieve their goals.
